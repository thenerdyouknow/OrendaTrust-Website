Orenda Trust is a non-profit organisation which aims to empower youth to make a positive change in their own lives and in the lives of others.

OUR PROJECTS

PROJECT CRAFT

Project CrAFT (Creating Awareness for Tomorrow) was our first project, and concluded in 2018. Through workshops, campaigns, and training sessions, we worked on improving life skills education and socio-environmental awareness amongst underprivileged children.

PROJECT SAATH

Project Saath centres on building a community of mental health through support and solidarity. We focus on providing not just support for and destigmatisation of mental illness, but also aim to promote overall mental well-being as a whole.
